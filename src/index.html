About the Artist

Alondra Monserrat is a multidisciplinary artist whose work explores the intricate relationship between memory, identity, and the natural world. Through a blend of painting, sculpture, and digital media, she creates evocative pieces that invite viewers to reflect on their own personal histories and connections to the environment.

Drawing inspiration from her cultural heritage and the vibrant landscapes of Mexico, Alondra's art is characterized by its rich textures, dynamic use of color, and symbolic imagery. Her process often involves layering different materials and techniques, a method that mirrors the way memories are built and transformed over time. Each piece is a journey into the self, a dialogue between the past and present, and a celebration of the human spirit's resilience.
